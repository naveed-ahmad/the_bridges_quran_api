101- Al-Qâri‘ah

1 The Shocking Event! 2 What is the Shocking Event? 3 And what can make yousg realize what the Shocking Event is? 4 A day when mankind become like scattered moths, 5 and the mountains become like tufted wool. 6 So, as for the one whose scales were heavy[1918]— 7 he is in a pleasant life. 8 But as for the one whose scales were lightb— 9 his home[1919] is an Abyss. 10 And what can make yousg realize what it is? 11 A raging fire.
