102- At-Takâthur

1 Vying for more distracted youpl 2 until you visited the graves. 3 But no! Youpl will come to know. 4 Once again: But no! Youpl will come to know. 5 But no! If only youpl knew with the knowledge of certitude: 6 you will certainly see[1920] purgatory. 7 Then youpl will certainly see it with the eye of certitude. 8 Then, on that day, youpl will certainly be asked about (worldly) bliss.
