93- Ad-Dhuhâ

1 By the morning shine. 2 By the night as it settles— 3 your Lord has not abandoned yousg, nor has He despised (you). 4 And indeed the Last is better for yousg than the First, 5 and your Lord will certainly give you so that you will be well pleased. 6 Did He not find yousg an orphan, so He sheltered (you); 7 and found you wandering, so He guided (you); 8 and found you in need, so He enriched (you)? 9 So as for the orphan, dosg not subdue; 10 and as for the asker, do not rebuke; 11 and as for the blessing of your Lord, do talk about it.
