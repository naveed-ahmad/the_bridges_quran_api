107- Al-Mâ‘ûn

1 Have yousg seen the one who denies the Recompense[1923]? 2 It is the one who mistreats the orphan 3 and does not urge (others to serve) food to the destitutesg. 4 So woe to the ones who pray— 5 those who are heedless of their prayers, 6 those who make show (of their deeds) 7 yet withhold (little) kindnesses[1924].
