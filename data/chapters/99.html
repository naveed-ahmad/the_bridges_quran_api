99- Al-Zalzalah

1 When the earth is made to quake with its earthquake, 2 and the earth brings out its heavy burdens, 3 and man says, “What is the matter with it?”— 4 On that day, it tells its tales, 5 because yoursg Lord has revealed to it. 6 On that day, mankind emerge in droves to be shown their deeds. 7 So whoever does an atom’s weight of good sees it, 8 and whoever does an atom’s weight of evil sees it.
