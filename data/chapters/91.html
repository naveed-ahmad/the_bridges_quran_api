91- Ash-Shams

1 By the sun and its morning shine, 2 and the moon as it follows her, 3 and the daytime as it reveals her, 4 and the night as it conceals her. 5 By the sky and He Who built it. 6 By the land and He Who spread it. 7 By the (inner) self and He Who formed it 8 then inspired it its own wickedness and its own mindfulness (of God)— 9 surely, successful is the one who has purified it, 10 and surely, failing is the one who has stunted it. 11 Exceeding all limits caused Thamûd[1902] to disbelieve, 12 when the most wicked man among them was delegated. 13 Then the messenger of Allah[1903] said to them, “This is the she-camel of Allah, so let her drink.” 14 So they disbelieved him and hamstrung her, so their Lord crushed them for their misdeed and leveled it[1904], 15 and[1905] He does[1906] not fear its ultimate outcome.
