104- Al-Humazah

1 Woe to every slandering backbiter, 2 who hoarded[1921] wealth and counted it over, 3 thinking that his wealth has made him immortal. 4 But no! He will surely be thrown into the Crusher. 5 And what can make you realize what the Crusher is? 6 Allah’s kindled Fire 7 that mounts over burning hearts. 8 Indeed, it is over them with no exit, 9 in extended columns.
