95- At-Tîn

1 By the fig and the olive. 2 By Mount Tûr of Sinai. 3 By this safe city[1910]— 4 surely, We created man in the best state, 5 then We returned him to the lowest of the low, 6 except those who attained faith and did righteous deeds, for for them is an unfailing reward. 7 So what makes yousg still deny the recompense? 8 Is Allah not the Wisest of judges?
