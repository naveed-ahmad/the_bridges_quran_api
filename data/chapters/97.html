97- Al-Qadr

1 We sent it[1911] down on the Night of Determination. 2 But what can make yousg realize what the Night of Determination is? 3 The Night of Determination is better than a thousand months— 4 in it descend the angels, with the Spirit[1912] among them, by the leave of their Lord with every command. 5 Peace! It is until the rise[1913] of dawn.
