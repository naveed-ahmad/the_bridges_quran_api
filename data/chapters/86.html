86- At-Târiq

1 By the sky and the pulsar. 2 But what can make yousg realize what the pulsar is? 3 The Drilling Star. 4 There is no self but has[1883] a preserver over it. 5 Let man look at what he was created from. 6 He was created from an ejected fluid, 7 coming out from between the backbone and the breastbones. 8 He is certainly able to return him 9 on the day when the secrets are put on trial, 10 then he will have neither strength nor helper. 11 By the sky that returns[1884]. 12 By the earth that cracks open— 13 it is most surely a decisive word[1885], 14 and it is no joke. 15 Indeed, they are plotting plots, 16 but I plot plots (as well). 17 So givesg the deniers respite; respite them briefly.
