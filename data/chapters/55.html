55- Ar-Rahmân

1 The All-Merciful 2 taught the Recitala, 3 created man, 4 and taught him clear expression. 5 The sun and the moon move according to plan, 6 and the plants[1676] and the trees prostrate themselves. 7 And the sky—He raised it and set up the balance, 8 so dopl not exceed in the balance, 9 but maintain the weights justly and do not fall short in the balance. 10 And the earth—He set it up for living creatures; 11 in it are fruits and sheathed palm trees, 12 and grains[1677] in the blades, and fragrant plants. 13 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in[1678]?14 He created man from potter’s clay like pottery 15 and created the unseen being[1679] from a smokeless flame of fire. 16 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 17 Lord of the two Easts and Lord of the two Wests. 18 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 19 He merged the two waters[1680], converging together; 20 between them is a barrier which they do not overrun. 21 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 22 From them come out[1681] pearls and corals. 23 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in?  24 His are the running ships, whose sails are raised[1682] in the sea like banners[1683]. 25 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 26 Everyone upon it[1684] is bound to perish, 27 and all that remains is yoursg Lord’s Countenance, the Possessor of majesty and nobility. 28 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 29 Everyone in the heavens and the earth asks Him; every day He is bringing about a matter. 30 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 31 We[1685] will attend to youpl, O two huge communitiesc. 32 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 33 O society of unseen beings and humans, if youpl can penetrate the bounds of the heavens and the earth then go ahead and penetrate; you will never penetrate except with authority[1686]. 34 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 35 Flares of fire, and brass[1687], will be sent upon youdl, and you will not find help. 36 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 37 And when the sky ruptures and becomes rose-colored like oil— 38 so which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 39 On that day neither any human nor unseen being[1688] is asked about his misdeeds. 40 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 41 The criminals are recognized by their marks then taken by the forelocks and the feet. 42 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 43 This is Hell in which the criminals disbelieve; 44 they circulate between it and an intensely scalding water. 45 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in?  46 But for whoever feared standing before his Lord there are two Gardens (in Paradise). 47 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 48 Both having varieties. 49 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 50 In them are two flowing springs. 51 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 52In them, of every kind of fruit there is a pair. 53So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 54 Reclining on furnishings lined with brocade, and the yield of the two Gardens are near at hand. 55 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 56 In them are bashful maidens, untouched before by any human or unseen beingb. 57 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 58 Like rubies and corals. 59 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 60 Is the repayment of kindness anything but kindness? 61 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in?  62 And beneath them are two gardens. 63 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 64 Both of deepest green. 65 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 66 In them are two gushing springs. 67 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 68 In them are fruits and date-palms and pomegranates. 69 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 70 In them are good, beautiful ones. 71 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 72 Spouses with lovely wide eyes, secluded in pavilions. 73 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 74 Untouched before by any human or unseen beingb. 75 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 76 Reclining on green cushions and exquisite carpets. 77 So which of your Lord’s marvels do youdl disbelieve in? 78 Supremely Blessed be the Name of yoursg Lord, the Possessor[1689] of majesty and nobility.
