37- As-Sâffât

1 By those aligned, lined up in ranks. 2 Then by the propellers propelling firmly. 3 Then by the readers of a Reminder— 4 indeed, yourpl God is surely One, 5 Lord of the heavens and the earth and whatever is between them, and Lord of the Easts. 6 Indeed, We have adorned the lowest[1386] heaven with an adornment, the planets[1387], 7 and as a protection against every defiant devil. 8 They cannot eavesdrop[1388] on the Higher Assembly, for they get bombarded from every side— 9 repelled, and they will have an unremitting punishment, 10 except for one who snatched a fragment and was then pursued by a piercing meteor. 11 So inquiresg of them, “Are they more difficult to create, or the others We created?” Indeed, We created them from sticky clay. 12 Rather, yousg were amazed[1389] as they ridicule. 13 And when reminded, they remember not. 14 And when they see a sign, they exaggerate in ridiculing 15 and say, “This is nothing but evident sorcery. 16 Can it be that when we have died and become dust and bones, can it be that[1390] we will really be resurrected? 17 And[1391] our ancient forefathers?” 18 Saysg, “Yes indeed, and you will be totally subdued.” 19 It is but a single nudge, and there they are looking on (in bewilderment)! 20 And they said[1392], “Woe to us! This is the Day of Recompense.” 21 “This is the Day of Decision which youpl used to deny. 22 Herdpl those who were unjust and their spouses, and what they used to worship 23 apart from Allah, and guide them to the path to purgatory. 24 And stop them—indeed, they are to be questioned.” 25 “What is the matter with youpl that you do not help one another?” 26 Rather, on this day, they are submissive. 27 And they cameg to one another, asking one another. 28 They[1393] said, “Indeed, youpl used to come at us from the right[1394]” 29 They[1395] said, “Rather, youpl yourselves were not believers, 30 and we had no authority over you; rather, you yourselves were a people who exceeded all limits. 31 And therefore we deserved our Lord’s decree on us. Most surely we will all get a taste, 32 for we deluded youpl; indeed, we were deluded.” 33 So on that day, they are indeed partners in the punishment. 34 This is what We do with the criminals. 35 Indeed, when it was said to them, “There is no god except Allah,” they would act arrogantly 36 and say, “Are we really to abandon our gods for a mad poet?” 37 In fact, he came with the truth, and he confirmed the emissaries. 38 Most surely, youpl will taste the painful punishment, 39 and in no way will you be repaid except for what you used to do, 40 except for Allah’s sincere chosen[1396] servants. 41 For those, there is a known provision: 42 fruits, and they are honored 43 in the Gardens of Bliss, 44 on couches facing one another. 45 A drink is passed round among them from a flowing spring: 46 white, a delight to those who drink, 47 no headiness in it, nor by it are they[1397] drained[1398]. 48 And with them are bashful maidens with lovely eyes, 49 as if they were closely guarded eggs. 50 So they came[1399] to one another, asking one another. 51 One of them said, “Indeed, I did have a comrade 52 who would say, ‘Can it be that yousg are really of those who believe? 53 Can it be that when we have died and become dust and bones, can it be that[1400] we will really be called to account?’” 54 He said (to his companions in Paradise), “Can youpl view (him)?” 55 Then he viewed (him) and saw him in the midst of purgatory. 56 He said, “(I swear) by Allah, yousg almost ruined me. 57 And were it not for the blessing of my Lord, I would have been of the arraigned.” 58 “Are we (then) not to die 59 except for our first death? And are we never to be punished?” 60 It is most surely this which is the great triumph. 61 For the like of this let the workers work. 62 Is this a better hospitality or the Tree of Zaqqum[1401]? 63 Indeed, We have made it a means of trial for the unjust. 64 Indeed, it is a tree that comes out from the very origin of purgatory. 65 Its yield is as if it were devils’ heads. 66 So they are sure to eat from it and fill their bellies with it. 67 Then, on top of it, they will have a brew of scalding water. 68 Then their return is sure to be to purgatory. 69 Indeed, they found their forefathers astray, 70 so they rush along in their footsteps. 71 And certainly, most of the ancients before them went astray. 72 And certainly, We sent among them forewarners. 73 So looksg at what the outcome was for those who were warned, 74 except for Allah’s sincere chosen[1402] servants. 75 And certainly, Noah called out to Us, so the very excellent of responders are We! 76 Thus We delivered him and his family from the great calamity, 77 and We made his offspring the sole survivors, 78 and We left him thus to be remembered among later generations. 79 Peace be upon Noah among (all creatures of) all realms. 80 Indeed, thus do We repay those who excel in doing good. 81 Indeed, he is one of Our believing servants. 82 Then We drowned the others. 83 And, indeed, of his kind is Abraham. 84 Recall when he came to his Lord with a sound heart. 85 He said to his father and his people, “What are you worshiping? 86 Is it false gods, instead of Allah, that youpl want? 87 So what is yourpl opinion about the Lord of all realms?” 88 Then he took one look at the stars 89 and said, “I am sick.” 90 So they turned away from him, fleeing, 91 whereupon he turned to their gods and said, “Will youpl not eat? 92 What is it with youpl? Do you not speak?” 93 Then he turned on them, striking with his right hand. 94 So they came towards him, dashing[1403]. 95 He said, “Do you worship what you carve, 96 when Allah has created youpl and all that you do?” 97 They said, “Build a structure for him and throw him into purgatory.” 98 So they desired to plot against him, but We made them the lowest. 99 And then he said, “I am going to my Lord—He will guide me.” 100 “My Lord, give me one of the righteous.” 101 So We gave him glad tidings of a forbearing boy. 102 Then, when he was old enough to accompany him, he said, “O my child, I see in a dream that I am sacrificing you, so look—what do you see[1404]?” He said, “O my father, do as you are commanded; you will find me, Allah willing, of the patient ones.” 103 Then, when theydl had submitted and he laid him down on his forehead, 104 We then called out to him, “O Abraham! 105 Yousg have truly fulfilled the vision.” Thus indeed do We repay those who excel in doing good. 106 Indeed, this was certainly the evident test. 107 And We redeemed him with a great sacrifice, 108 and We left him thus to be remembered among later generations: 109 “Peace be upon Abraham.” 110 Thus We repay those who excel in doing good. 111 Indeed, he is one of Our believing servants. 112 And We gave him glad tidings of Isaac, a prophet, one of the righteous. 113 And We blessed him and Isaac. But of their offspring is one who excels in doing good and one who is evidently unjust to himself. 114 And We certainly conferred Our favor upon Moses and Aaron, 115 and We delivered themdl and their people from the great calamity. 116 And We supported thempl, so they were the overcomers. 117 And We gave themdl the evidential Scripture, 118 and We guided themdl upon the straight path. 119 And We left themdl thus to be remembered among later generations: 120 “Peace be upon Moses and Aaron.” 121 Thus do We repay those who excel in doing good. 122 Indeed, they are of Our believing servants. 123 And Elijah is indeed one of the emissaries. 124 Recall when he said to his people, “Will you not be mindful (of God)? 125 Do youpl call upon Baʻl[1405] and leave the best of creators— 126 Allah, your Lord[1406] and the Lord of your ancient fathers?” 127 But they disbelieved him, and thus they will certainly be arraigned, 128 except for Allah’s sincere chosen[1407] servants. 129 And We left him thus to be remembered among later generations: 130 “Peace be upon Elijah[1408].” 131 Thus do We repay those who excel in doing good. 132 Indeed, he is one of Our believing servants. 133 And Lot is indeed one of the emissaries. 134 Recall when We delivered him and all of his family, 135 except for an old woman[1409] who lingered behind. 136 Then We annihilated the others. 137 And most surely youpl pass by them in the morning 138 and at night. Do you not reason? 139 And Jonah is indeed one of the emissaries. 140 Recall when he fled to the overloaded ship, 141 and he drew lots and was of those who lost. 142 Then the whale took him into its mouth, and he was to blame. 143 And had he not been one of those who highly exalt God, 144 he would have stayed in its belly until the day they are resurrected. 145 Then We threw him into the wilderness while he was sick, 146 and We germinated a gourd tree over him. 147 Then We sent him to a hundred thousand or more. 148 And they attained faith, so We gave them enjoyment for some time. 149 So inquiresg of them, “Are the daughters for yoursg Lord while for them are the sons?” 150 Or did We create the angels as females while they witnessed?” 151 Undoubtedly, it is out of their utter falsehood that they do indeed say, 152 “Allah has begotten.” But they are most surely liars. 153 Has He selected daughters over sons? 154 What is it with youpl? How do you judge? 155 Will you not remind[1410] yourselves? 156 Or do youpl have an evident manifestation? 157 Then bring yourpl scripture, if you have been truthful. 158 And they invented a relationship between Him and (the race of) unseen beings[1411]. And certainly, (the race of) unseen beings knew that they (who made the claims) would surely be arraigned— 159 Highly Exalted is Allah far above what they describe— 160 except for Allah’s sincere chosen[1412] servants. 161 Thus surely, youpl and what you worship 162 can never seduce (anyone) away from Him, 163 except for one who is to roast in purgatory. 164 “And there is not one of us[1413] but has an assigned position. 165 And we are indeed the aligned. 166 And we are indeed those who exalt God.” 167 Even though they[1414] used to say, 168 “Had we received a Reminder from the ancients[1415], 169 we would have been Allah’s sincere chosen servants.” 170 But they denied it, so they will find out. 171 And most surely Our Word has already gone out to our servant emissaries: 172 indeed, it is they who shall surely be supported, 173 and indeed it is Our soldiers who shall surely be the overcomers. 174 So turnsg away from them for some time, 175 and watch them—for they will soon see. 176 Do they seek to hasten Our punishment? 177 Yet, when it descends into their courtyard, miserable shall be the morning of those forewarned[1416]. 178 And turnsg away from them for a while, 179 and watch—they will soon see. 180 Highly Exalted is your Lord, the Lord of Might, far above what they describe. 181 And peace be upon the emissaries. 182 And all praise be to Allah, Lord of all realms.
