We[468] will repay those who keep turning away from Our signs with the worst kind of punishment on account of how they used to constantly turn away. 158 Are they waiting for anything but for the angels to come to them or for your Lord to come or for some of your Lord’s signs to come? On the day when some of your Lord’s signs come, no self will benefit from attaining faith unless it had attained faith previously or had earned goodness through its faith. Saysg, “Wait; indeed, we too are waiting.” 159 Indeed, the ones who have caused disunity in[469] their religion and became sects—yousg have nothing to do with them. Rather, their case rests with Allah, then He informs them of what they used to do. 160 Whoever has come up with a good deed—for him there is ten times its like[470], and whoever has come up with an evil deed will be repaid only with its equivalent, and they will not be dealt with unjustly.  161 Say, “Surely (as for) myself, my Lord has guided me to a straight path—an upright religion[471]—the creed of Abraham who turned away from all that is false, and never was he one of the polytheists.” 162 Say, “Indeed, my prayer and my rites and my way of living and my way of dying are for Allah, Lord of all realms. 163 No associate is there for Him, and to this I have been commanded, and I am the first of the Muslims[472]. 164 Say, “Am I to seek a Lord other than Allah when He is the Lord of everything?” And not a single self earns except what it is due, and no bearer of burden bears the burden of another. Then to yourpl Lord is your return, then He will inform you regarding whatever you used to differ over. 165 And it is He Who made you successors on the earth and elevated some of you by degrees over others in order to test you through what He has brought you. Indeed, yoursg Lord is Swift in punishment, and He is most surely Oft-Forgiving, Bestowing of mercy.
